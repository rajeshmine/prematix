CM550S Printable Handheld Terminal with Android Fingerprint Recognition

CM550S series of products are printable hand-held terminals with Android fingerprint recognition, equipping with 1D and 2D scanning module, NFC, 900M RFID-UHF, fingerprint recognition, thermal printing module and power infrared module. Meanwhile, CM550S can realize the simultaneous configuration of bar code scanning and 900M UHF RFID, fully showing the high integration of hand-held terminal. CM550S can effectively meet the diversified operating needs of customers.

Product Story

CM550S is able to support 1d/2d laser barcode scanning head, NFC reader, 900M RFID-UHF, fingerprint identification, thermal printer and power infrared module. At the same time, CM550S supports to integrate barcode scanning and 900M UHF RFID integrate in one which show all in one of handheld terminal perfectly. It is the most reliable mobile hand held equipment during enterprise information work.
