Product Story

CT80 is able to store barcodes with aggressive capacity of 2MB memory. Adopting 2.4G+BLE two modes ( BLE represents the low power consumption way of Bluetooth), the ultra-low wireless power and radiance will not do harm to human body. All the common systems are able to be paired with high efficiency. In addition, featured with pair and scan (2.4G) and connection without password (BT4.0), CT80 supports plug and play to laptop or desktop computer through 2.4G. In terms of mobile phones and tablets, CT80 can pair with them with one-click and without password, supporting scanning 1d/2d barcodes on the screens, mobile phones and computers.
