ELON MUSK: THE TIME TRAVELLER

By Elon’s magic 30 Comments

Who’s not familiar with the name Elon Musk? Elon Musk whose name is synonymous with big brands like Tesla, SpaceX, Solar city and recently, The Boring Company. From Electric self-driving cars to Human settlements on Mars, makes you wonder whether he’s from the Present or a time travelling wizard hell-bent on helping man kind. And he recently announced a new company which he calls ‘The Boring Company’ which makes 3D underground tunnels to reduce traffic congestion. And he recently unveiled a new semi-truck that runs on a battery with superior range than the average diesel trucks.

The Hyperloop project, which Elon claims to be faster than bullet train has set its sight on Amaravathi, the new capital of Andhra Pradesh which will receive the first Hyperloop track connecting Vijayawada with Amaravathi. And Andhra Pradesh will be the first state in India to receive it.

Elon also talks about futuristic solar roofing which would be seen in the near future. A source of renewable and clean energy which American households can now produce and use themselves rather than depending on the power grid. All these recent innovations prove that Musk is an outer world alien or a time traveller from the future. None of these technologies would have seen the light of the day if not for Elon Musk.

The Time Travelling wizard is just getting started, as he has more tricks up his sleeve and the world will see more of Elon’s magic.

Will he be the face of change in technology disrupting the Human Ways?
